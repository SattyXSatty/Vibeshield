# 💬 Integrating with Chat

VibeShield doesn't just watch your code — it understands your intent by analyzing your conversations with the built-in AI assistant.

### How to use it:
1. Open the VibeShield view in the **Activity Bar** (on the left side of your editor).
2. Chat with the AI about the features you are building or the bugs you are fixing.
3. Click **Extract Intent** in the VibeShield Overlay UI. The Cortex-R engine will automatically read your recent chat history and combine it with your uncommitted git diffs to figure out what needs to be tested.

> **💡 Pro Tip:** The more detailed you are in your chat about *how* a feature should work, the better VibeShield will be at writing accurate, autonomous UI tests for it.
